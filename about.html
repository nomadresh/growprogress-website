About Us

We understand the needs of the movement
because we come from the movement.

We are a team of progressive strategists, advocates, scientists, engineers, and activists, fluent in the languages of politics and advocacy. We know the challenges of persuasion firsthand and are passionate about building tools that help campaigns, causes, and unions win.

Grow Progress is a full persuasion platform: Audience Understanding to map who's persuadable before you spend, Rapid Message Testing to find what moves people in as little as 24 hours, Pulse Surveys for fast reads between research moments, and the Persuasion Library with 8,000+ tested messages. We built each product because we lived the problem it solves.

We are proud of the pace at which progressive campaigns and advocacy organizations have adopted our tools. You can find Grow Progress being used around the country by nonprofits, labor unions, state party committees, advocacy organizations, funders, independent expenditure campaigns, and research firms.
