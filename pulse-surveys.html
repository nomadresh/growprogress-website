What It Is

A rapid temperature check fielded in hours.

A Pulse Survey is a short (5–10 questions), fast-turnaround study designed to capture real-time opinion on a breaking moment. Unlike a full RMT, these aren't designed to measure causal persuasion, they measure current sentiment, salience, and reaction.

They answer the questions your team is asking right now:

"How are voters responding to this news?"
"Is this attack landing?"
"Do our supporters understand what just happened?"

Pulse vs RMT

Two tools. Different jobs.

Pulse Surveys and Rapid Message Tests work best together, not as substitutes for each other.

Pulse Survey
  • Lightweight, 5–10 questions
  • Measures current sentiment
  • Descriptive, what people think right now
  • 500–800 respondents
vs
Rapid Message Testing (RMT)
  • Rigorous, full RCT methodology
  • Measures causal persuasion
  • Prescriptive, what changes minds
  • Actionable for creative + media decisions
