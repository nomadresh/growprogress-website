Audience Targeting

Test with the exact voters
your campaign needs to move.

Generic national samples won't tell you how your district responds. Test with the exact audience that actually matters.

District-level

By congressional district, state senate seat, or county, results that reflect your actual electorate, not a national average.

Party & registration targeting

Test with Democrats, Republicans or independents.

Demographic sub-groups

The message that works for suburban women may backfire with rural men. Know before it runs by age, gender, race, education, and more.
